# Reference Corpus

Place **benchmark articles** here for structure and genre convention extraction.

- **Do not** paste competitor or client text you do not have rights to reuse.
- **Do not** copy benchmark prose into `draft.md` — use `extract_genre_conventions.py` and `audit_near_duplicate.py` instead.

## Layout

```text
content/reference/<topic-slug>/<article-type>/*.md
```

Article types: `blog`, `how-to`, `case-study`, `comparison`, `white-paper`.

See [`standards/reference_corpus_contract.md`](../../standards/reference_corpus_contract.md) in the bundle (or your installed copy) for the full contract.
